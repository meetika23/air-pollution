Air Quality Index Predictor

In the process of making countries developed, the environment is often neglected. Various inventions made to help as well as provide comfort to mankind such as automobiles, air conditioners, etc. led to the deterioration of the environment and the air quality. The major air pollutants are Carbon Monoxide (CO), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), particulate materials (PM2.5, SPM, and RSPM), Sulphur Dioxide (SO2), greenhouse gases, and Ozone(O3). These gases cause various respiratory diseases (such as asthma, and pulmonary cancer), Leukemia, Mesothelioma (a type of lung cancer), and premature deaths.

How it works?

This web application predicts the Air Quality Index (AQI) by taking in levels of four major air pollutants - Sulphur dioxide (SO2), Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Respirable Suspended Particulate Matter (RSPM), and Suspended Particulate Matter (SPM). To make these predictions, this application uses four supervised machine learning algorithms such as Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, Random Forest and Decision Tree to analyze the pollutant levels and predict how they will impact air quality.
